Shrimp Rice Bowl Recipe

Description

This is a very simpel sushi recipe, it has been passed down from generations.

Ingredientes

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 small cucumber (cut into thin strips)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 small carrot (optional, julienned)
  • 4 oz salmon, crab, or your choice of protein
  • Soy sauce (for dipping)
  • Bamboo sushi mat (optional but helps roll neatly)

Steps

1. Cook the rice

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook with water (2:2.5 ratio) in a rice cooker or pot.
  3. Let it cool slightly.

2. Season the rice

  1. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small pan — heat until dissolved.
  2. Stir into warm rice. Let it cool to room temperature.

3. Prepare fillings

  1. Slice cucumber, avocado, and other fillings into thin strips.
  2. Have your fish or protein ready.

4. Assemble the roll

  1. Place a nori sheet shiny side down on your bamboo mat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving 1 inch uncovered at the top.
  3. Arrange fillings (fish, cucumber, avocado) horizontally across the center.

5. Roll the sushi

  1. Lift the edge of the mat closest to you and roll it forward, pressing gently to shape it into a log.
  2. Use a bit of water on the top edge to seal the roll.

6. Cut and serve

  1. Slice the roll into 6-8 pieces using a sharp, wet knife.
  2. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
